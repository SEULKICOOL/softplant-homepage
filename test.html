What is our design process?

We follow a detailed process when working with you, grounded in the Double Diamond Design Process. This method, popularized by the British Design Council in 2005, has also been adopted by top UX design agencies like IDEO and Nielsen Norman. The goal is to align everyone on the same wavelength, enhancing both communication efficiency and the quality of deliverables.

Step #1 | Discover: To accurately identify challenges, our lead UX designer conducts both qualitative and quantitative research to understand the underlying user pain points and needs we aim to address.

Step #2 | Define: We uncover the research data through comprehensive analysis, pinpointing key focus areas. Subsequently, we define goals, user journeys, and experiences based on these insights, which guide us towards the right solutions.

Step #3 | Design: At this stage, we diverge, using creativity and data to create aesthetically pleasing user interfaces &experiences tailored to your targeted customers.

Step #4 | Constantly Test &Improve: Finally, we converge again. Our focus shifts to delivering solutions that effectively address user needs and pain points. This is achieved through the development of prototypes, user testing, and a commitment to continuous improvement of the existing digital product.
